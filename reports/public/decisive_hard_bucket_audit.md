Small and noisy; keep as supporting evidence only:

## Hardest Stable Cluster

The persistent hard region in the completed study is best described as:
- `square`
- imported data, especially `imported_sg`
- inverse/high-contrast appearance pockets

## Practical Reading

The hard-bucket effect is not just a tiny-bucket artifact. It is strongest and most defensible for `square` and `imported_sg`.
