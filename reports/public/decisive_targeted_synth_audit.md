# Decisive Targeted Synthetic Audit

## Branch Tested

A matched-budget targeted synthetic branch focused on the hard square bucket was compared against the completed global `synthetic_static` baseline.

## Result

The targeted branch showed a brief early advantage but did not maintain it through the useful mid-training regime.

At the public-summary level, the verdict is simple:
- targeted synthetic did not produce a stronger or more mature result than completed global static mixing;
- the strongest claim should therefore not be a targeted-synthetic method winner.

## What This Adds

This is a useful negative result:
- it weakens a schedule/method-centric framing;
- it strengthens the interpretation that the main contribution is subgroup-aware evaluation and hard-bucket analysis, not another training trick.
